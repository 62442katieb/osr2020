Schedule

The OSR program runs from 22 June 2020 to 3 July 2020, during the same 2-week period as the virtual OHBM meeting program. There is minimal overlap between the OHBM and OSR programs to allow you to attend both. For increased accessibility, the daily OSR schedule runs 3 times per 24h, once per time zone hub.
